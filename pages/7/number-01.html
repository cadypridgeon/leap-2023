The Number System

Introduction

Previously you were introduced to whole numbers and integers which are an important part of the number system. Today we will be exploring fractions and decimals, an important part of the number system, and their relationships to one another.

Definitions

  • Decimal: A decimal is a number that includes a decimal point that is represented as '.' and has a number to the left and right of the decimal point.
  • Fraction: A fraction is a number that can be written as a/b where a and b are both whole numbers but a/b is not a whole number.

Example

Write the fraction 3/4 as a decimal.

Solution

The answer is 0.75. To convert 3/4 to a decimal you divide 3 by 4. To do this on paper, you will use your longer division skills and eventually you will come to 0.75.
