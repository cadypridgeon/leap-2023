The Number System

Introduction

The number system is all around us and understanding how it works is critical to solving various math problems. We will be exploring what whole numbers and integers are and how they can be used in the real world.

Definitions

  • Whole Numbers: Whole numbers are a set of numbers from zero and up that represent complete values (0, 1, 2, 3, ...)
  • Integers: Integers include whole numbers but also include negative numbers (..., -3, -2, -1, 0, 1, 2, 3, ...)

Example

If you have one apple and eat half of it for a snack, do you still have a whole apple?

Solution

No, you have half an apple left. Whole numbers represent complete quantities and do not include incomplete parts like halves.
