Our Principles

LEAP, or the Learning Explorations Accelerated Program, is all about tackling the unique challenges brought on by the COVID-19 pandemic and the shift to online learning.

We're laser-focused on enriching math education through a dynamic online platform. With e-Learning practices in mind, we've created a system that's tailored for students from Pre-K to 12th grade, meeting both state and national education standards.

With recent national assessments showing lower math and reading scores for thirteen-year-olds, we're determined to make a positive change. Our platform offers grade-specific courses that can be personalized to each student's learning style.

At LEAP, we're working towards a future where every student can thrive, no matter where or how they're learning.
